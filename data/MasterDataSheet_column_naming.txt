Column name description

Participant: (P***) Unique id
Sex: (Female/Male)
Age: (years)
Height: (m)
Mass: (kg)
RunningDaysAWeek: (days)
KmAWeek: (km)
Injuries12Months: reported by participants
Time10K: (hh:mm:ss) reported by participants
LT: (km/h) lactate threshold speed calculated during maximal incremental protocol
VO2max: (ml/min/kg) VO2peak normalised to body bass reached during maximal incremental protocol
RQ: (VCO2/VO2) respiratory quotient achieved during maximal incremental protocol
HRmax: (bpm) peak heart rate achieved during maximal incremental protocol
RPEmax: (Borgs scale 6-20) peak rate of perceived exertion achieved during maximal incremental protocol
MaxCompletedSpeed: (km/h) during maximal incremental protocol
MaxStartedSpeed: (km/h) during maximal incremental protocol
Temperature: (°C) lab temperature during maximal incremental protocol
Humidity: (%) in the lab during maximal incremental protocol
Pressure: (mmhg) in the lab during maximal incremental protocol
EE10-20: (kcal/min) energy expenditure using Weir's equation at speeds 10 through to 20 km/h during maximal incremental protocol. Note that this is only reported for submaximal speeds (below LT)
VO10-20: (ml/min/kg) VO2 normalised to body mass at speeds 10 through to 20 km/h during maximal incremental protocol. Note that this is only reported for submaximal speeds (below LT)
HR10-20: (bpm) heart rate at speeds 10 through to 20 km/h during maximal incremental protocol. Note that this is only reported for submaximal speeds (below LT)
PelvWidth: (m) pelvis width measured as the distance between the two hip joint centres during the static calibration trial.
ThiLgth_r: (m) right thigh length measured as the distance between the hip and knee joint centres during the static calibration trial.
ThiLgth_l: (m) left thigh length measured as the distance between the hip and knee joint centres during the static calibration trial.
ShaLgth_r: (m) right thigh length measured as the distance between the knee and ankle joint centres during the static calibration trial.
ShaLgth_l: (m) left thigh length measured as the distance between the knee and ankle joint centres during the static calibration trial.
LegLgth_r: (m) right leg length measured as the vertical position of the hip joint centre during the static calibration trial.
LegLgth_l: (m) left leg length measured as the vertical position of the hip joint centre during the static calibration trial.
FootLgth_r: (m) right foot length measured as the distance between the marker on the heel (RFCC) and the distal end of the big toe during the static calibration trial.
FootLgth_l: (m) left foot length measured as the distance between the marker on the heel (LFCC) and the distal end of the big toe during the static calibration trial.
TrunkLgth: (m) trunk length measured as participant's height - leg length during the static calibration trial.
Sess2_time: (hh:mm:ss) time run during the run to exhaustion at constant speed (session 2)
Sess2_RPE: (Borgs scale 6-20) rate of perceived exertion achieved at the end of the run to exhaustion at constant speed (session 2)
Sess2_La: (mmol/l) lactate in blood at the end of the run to exhaustion at constant speed (session 2)
Sess2_Temperature: (°C) lab temperature during long run to exhaustion (session 2)
Sess2_Humidity: (%) in the lab during long run to exhaustion (session 2)
Sess2_Pressure: (mmHg) in the lab during long run to exhaustion (session 2)
EEJW10-20: (kcal/min) energy expenditure in using Jeukendrup & Wallis's equation at speeds 10 through to 20 km/h during maximal incremental protocol. Note that this is only reported for submaximal speeds (below LT)
RPE10-25: (Borgs scale 6-20) rate of perceived exertion at speeds 10 through to 21 km/h during maximal incremental protocol. Note that this is only reported for those speeds which were started.
